Rock-Paper-Scissors

Are You Ready?

Instructions

You've been challenged to rock-paper-scissors by the smug bug! The rules are the classic rock paper scissors rules set with rock beating scissors, scissors beating paper, and paper beating rock. Click the start the match button to begin, and click on the option icons below the match area to make your choice of the trio (the default is rock). Once you've decided, hit the ready button and the winner of the match is revealed!

About

This game is a little more complicated than the guessing game, though not by much. The choice of the computer is completely random, so any perceived intelligence on the part of the computer is entirely in your head. The higher level of complexity in this one actually comes from tracking all the different statistics and deciding what the optimal structure was for the game. At first I was planning on implementing a 5 second timer, or something to that effect, but after various testing I opted just to let the player have control over when they are ready to proceed to the results.
